🚀 Human Resources Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

Transforming Workforce Data into Strategic Business Decisions

📌 Overview

This project is a comprehensive HR Analytics solution built in Power BI, designed to help organizations move beyond reporting and into data-driven workforce strategy.

It integrates Workforce, Performance, and Compensation analytics into a single executive dashboard — enabling leaders to monitor cost, risk, and productivity in real time.

📂 Dataset:

💰 Financial Intelligence

At its core, this dashboard is not just HR-focused — it is financially driven.

Total Payroll: $759M
Total Employees: ~9,000
Average Salary: $85K
Attrition Rate: 12%
Overtime Workforce: 30%
🔍 Key Financial Insight:

Even a 1% increase in attrition can result in multi-million dollar losses due to hiring, onboarding, and productivity gaps.

📊 Dashboard Architecture

The solution is structured into 4 executive-level pages, each serving a strategic purpose:

1️⃣ Overview (Executive Summary)
KPI Cards → Payroll, Headcount, Attrition, Overtime
Department Headcount → Workforce distribution
Gender & Education → Workforce composition
2️⃣ Workforce Analytics
Headcount by Department (Bar Chart)
Headcount by State (Matrix + % contribution)
Education Distribution (Bar Chart)

👉 Helps answer:
Where is our workforce concentrated?

3️⃣ Performance Analytics
Treemap → Performance by Education Level
Bar Chart → Performance by Department
KPI Metrics → Excellent, Good, Needs Improvement

👉 Helps answer:
Where are our top performers coming from?

4️⃣ Compensation & Risk Analysis
Salary Distribution (Donut Chart)
Avg Salary by Department (Bar Chart)
Attrition Matrix → Department-level risk
Overtime Breakdown → Operational stress

👉 Helps answer:
Where are we losing money and talent?

📈 Visualization Strategy

This dashboard is designed with decision-first thinking:

Visualization	Purpose
KPI Cards	Instant executive insights
Bar Charts	Department comparison
Donut Charts	Distribution analysis
Treemap	Hierarchical performance view
Matrix Table	Risk & detailed analysis

Every visual answers a specific business question, not just a data point.

🔍 Key Business Insights
Operations → Highest workforce + highest performance contribution
Finance & IT → Highest attrition (~13%) → Risk zones
30% Overtime → Indicates workload imbalance
Balanced Salary Distribution → Strong pay equity
Regional Concentration (72%) → Strategic dependency risk
🧠 Strategic Learnings
Attrition is not an HR metric — it is a financial leakage
Overtime is not productivity — it is inefficiency
Headcount is not growth — it is cost responsibility
